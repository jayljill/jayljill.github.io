Overview

Dr.storage is a successful company helping thousands of electronic factories throughout Asia. Dr.storage dedicates to humidity management that they can now offer an integrated solution that complies with J-STD-033D standard.

In this project, our goal is to create a website selling Dr.storage new products and attend IFA 2019 in Berlin. To be more specific, the website not just only represents their new product image but also for their salesperson promoting products. To support this idea, we used a bald color different than they used to create a new image. Furthermore, we create a purchase system that is more interactive for users.
